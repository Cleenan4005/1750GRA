Pudding á la Mode

Pudding la Mode is a delightful treat that combines the rich, creamy comfort of classic pudding with the sweet, chilly indulgence of ice cream. Each bite is a perfect balance of smooth, velvety texture and cool, dreamy flavor, making it a favorite for any occasion. Whether served warm or chilled, it’s a dessert that feels both nostalgic and special—simple yet elegant, and always satisfying.

Ingredients

For the pudding...

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp salt

For serving (á la mode)...

  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream (or your flavor of choice)
  • Any toppings of your choice (chocolate or caramel syrup, whipped cream, fresh fruit, nuts, ect.)
